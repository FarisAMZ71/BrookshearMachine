Command format:
{Command} {Target register}, {Bit pattern}

Commands (from brookshear):
LEA - load effective address
LDR - load register
STR - store
MOV - move
ADT - add 2's complement
ADF - add floating point
ORR - or
AND - and
XOR - xor
ROT - rotate
JMP - jump
HLT - halt
